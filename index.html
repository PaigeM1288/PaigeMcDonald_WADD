A Murder in Merivale

A Murder in Merivale is an interactive story game where you decide your own path.

Pay attention to the vital information on the police report and in your own alibi as it may just save you from a life sentence!

You will be interviewed by Detective Antony Bryant, A HARD-HEADED NO-NONSENSE IRON FISTof the law.

He will ask a question and you must answer accordingly using the options provided.

Good luck, you're going to need it!
